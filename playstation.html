PlayStation - Gaming with Kids

Overview

PlayStation, Sony's flagship gaming brand, has established itself as a leader in immersive console gaming with its PlayStation 5 and a legacy of innovative hardware like VR headsets. Renowned for exclusive blockbuster franchises like The Last of Us and God of War, Sony continues to dominate with high-quality, story-driven games. PlayStation Plus offers online multiplayer and a curated game library, while the brand remains a global icon for its commitment to cutting-edge technology and gaming experiences.

Parental Controls

PlayStation provides extensive parental controls for its consoles, including the PS5 and PS4. These controls let parents restrict games and media based on content ratings, limit screen time, and prevent unauthorized purchases. Communication settings can also be adjusted to block messages or voice chat from unknown players. To set up parental controls, create a child account under your PlayStation Network family management settings. On the console, go to Settings, select Family and Parental Controls, and choose the child account to manage. Adjust restrictions for content, spending, and online interactions. PlayStation also allows parents to set time limits and receive reports on gaming activity through the PlayStation app or website.
