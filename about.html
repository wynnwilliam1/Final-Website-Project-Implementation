About Us

Gaming with Kids

We here at Gaming with Kids (GwK) know what it's like to be a parent. We know how hard it can be to make sure youre kids are safe. In the modern world, technology has made that much more difficult, so we decided to step in. Gaming has been a passion of ours for years, and we want kids to safely experience the joy of gaming just like we all did growing up. That is our mission.

Founder

William Wynn grew up playing Nintendo 64, and has passionately played games since. He hopes to one day work within the industry, however possible, to spread his passion for gaming to as many people as he can reach.
