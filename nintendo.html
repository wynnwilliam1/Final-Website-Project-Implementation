Nintendo - Gaming with Kids

Overview

Nintendo is a pioneering gaming company recognized for its innovative consoles and beloved franchises like Mario, Zelda, and Pokémon. Its hybrid console, the Nintendo Switch, has redefined gaming with its portability and family-friendly appeal. Nintendo focuses on accessible and creative gaming experiences rather than cutting-edge graphics, fostering a unique niche in the industry. Through services like Nintendo Online and continued support for local multiplayer, Nintendo maintains its legacy as a cornerstone of gaming for all ages.

Parental Controls

Nintendo offers parental controls for the Switch through the console settings and the Nintendo Switch Parental Controls app. These tools allow parents to restrict game content based on ratings, limit playtime, and monitor gaming activity. Parents can also disable communication features like online chat and restrict purchases. To set up parental controls directly on the console, access the System Settings menu and select "Parental Controls." From there, choose the restrictions you’d like to apply, such as age-appropriate content or the ability to purchase items in the eShop. For more advanced controls, download the Parental Controls app on a smartphone, link it to the console, and configure playtime limits or suspend gameplay remotely when needed.
